# Introduction

A Vue drag and drop library.

A fast and lightweight drag & drop library for Vue.js with many configuration options, covering multiple scenarios.

All you have to do is import Container & Draggable into your project and have fun with it.

With a little help from our Callbacks, you can set up the right Events with the understanding of our Lifecycle.

Take a look at our examples here

Don't forget to use vue-devtools (opens new window) for a better experience
